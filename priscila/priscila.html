Cover letter

My journey on the realms of UX started in 2014, as an exchange student in Canada. In my classes at Kwantlen Polytechnic University I had my first contact with UX and started to scratch the surface of what it means. Years later, after a university thesis on the subject and one and a half years of market experience, I knew that yes, I really love it!

Right now I work with Sketch app, Invision, Adobe Illustrator and sometimes After Effects, and I have additionally 4 years of experience as Digital Designer in publicity and web design.

What I am looking forward right now are opportunities to learn and test processes, new challenges to study, solve and learn, new experiences!
